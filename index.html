The Problem

Farmers in Kathiawad face unpredictable weather, water scarcity, low crop yields, and lack of access to expert advice.

The Solution

KrishiAI provides AI-powered recommendations for crops, pest detection, irrigation scheduling, and market price forecasting.

Features

Crop Recommendation

Suggests the best crops to grow based on soil type, weather, and market demand.

Pest Detection

Identifies pests and diseases using smartphone photos.

Smart Irrigation

Optimizes irrigation schedules based on weather and soil moisture.

Market Price Forecasting

Predicts mandi prices to help farmers decide when and where to sell.

How It Works

Farmers take a photo of their crops, and KrishiAI identifies diseases or pests. It also uses weather and soil data to suggest when to water, what to plant, and when to sell.
